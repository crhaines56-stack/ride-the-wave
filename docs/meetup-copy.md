## GROUP DESCRIPTION

**Ride the Wave AI — Northwest Austin**

🌊 *To ensure our community rides the AI wave and is not crushed by it.*

---

AI is changing everything — how we work, how we run businesses, how we communicate. And it's moving fast.

If you live in Steiner Ranch, Lakeway, River Place, Four Points, or anywhere in northwest Austin, this group is for you.

**Ride the Wave AI is a free monthly meetup** where neighbors come together to:
- See real AI tools in action (no fluff, no hype)
- Share what's working in our own businesses and lives
- Ask the questions we're all too embarrassed to Google
- Help each other figure this stuff out — together

**You do NOT need a tech background.** You don't need to have used ChatGPT. You just need to be curious about how AI might help you, your business, or your family — and willing to learn from your neighbors.

### What to expect:
Every meeting is 90 minutes and follows a simple format:
- Open networking
- Welcome + mission (5 min)
- Main AI demo or talk (25 min)
- "What Are You Working On?" — member lightning shares (20 min)
- Open Q&A + group problem solving (15 min)
- Wrap-up + sign up to present next time

### Who comes:
Small business owners, freelancers, consultants, professionals, parents, retirees, curious neighbors. Everyone is welcome at every level.

### Who runs it:
Chris Haines — northwest Austin resident, father of four, and founder of Forge Agency (an AI consulting firm). Chris started this group because he kept having the same conversation with neighbors: "I know AI is important but I don't know where to start." This is his answer.

**Free. Monthly. No sales pitches.**

Come once. You'll be back.

## GROUP RULES

**Ride the Wave AI — Community Guidelines**

We want this to be the best AI meetup in Austin. These simple rules keep it that way:

**1. Be a good neighbor.**
This is a community group. Treat everyone with respect, curiosity, and patience. We're all learning.

**2. No sales pitches.**
You can mention your business when relevant and share what you do. But no pitches, no hard sells, no "book a call" pressure. Violations will result in removal.

**3. All experience levels welcome.**
"Dumb questions" don't exist here. The only dumb thing would be not asking. If someone makes another member feel stupid for their question, they're out.

**4. Share freely.**
If something's working for you, share it. That's the whole point. This community grows when members contribute.

**5. Keep it practical.**
We're here for real, actionable AI knowledge — not hype, not philosophy debates, not science fiction. What can you use today?

**6. No spam in the event comments.**
Sharing a relevant article or resource? Great. Posting your affiliate link or newsletter signup? Not here.

**7. Come to learn, stay to teach.**
After you've attended a few meetups, consider sharing something you've learned. A 5-minute demo can change someone's business.

**8. Keep the group local.**
This group is for northwest Austin neighbors. Remote-only members are welcome to follow along, but our focus is building a local community.
